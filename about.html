The Unconventional Path

My Journey

Not a straight line — and that's exactly the point.

2022 | The Switch

Left Commerce, Chose Code

In 2022 I made a bold decision — I switched from BCom to BCA, studying as a private student. No campus, no classroom. Just me, the internet, and a strong feeling that technology was where I needed to be.

2022 | YouTube Was My Classroom

Learning HTML, CSS & JS the Hard Way

I started from zero — watching YouTube tutorials for hours, building small projects, breaking things, and fixing them. HTML clicked first, then CSS, then the wild world of JavaScript. It was messy, slow, and absolutely worth it.

2022–2023 | The Great Confusion

Frontend or Backend? I Had No Idea

Every developer forum said something different. I tried both — built APIs, learned some Node.js, touched databases. The confusion was real. Eventually I chose to go deep into frontend first while keeping a basic understanding of how backends work.

2023 | ChatGPT Changed Everything

AI Became My Tutor

When ChatGPT arrived I used it differently from most — not as a shortcut, but as a teacher. I would paste my broken code and ask it to explain what went wrong and why. I also completed prompt engineering courses to get better at working with AI. This accelerated my learning dramatically.

2023–2024 | Real World

7-Month Internship at ARIES Nainital

I landed a 7-month internship at ARIES (Aryabhatta Research Institute of Observational Sciences) in Nainital. I worked with Python and Odoo — an open-source ERP system. This was my first real professional experience: actual deadlines, real codebases, and working in a team environment. It pushed me back into backend territory and taught me a lot about how real software is built.

2025–2026 | Going Deep into AI Agents

Agentic AI — The Next Frontier

After my internship I went all-in on AI-powered development. I started using Ollama to run local LLMs on my own machine, integrated Continue as an AI extension in VS Code, and switched to Cursor as my primary AI-native IDE. In 2026 I started using Antigravity for agentic project building. AI is no longer just a tool — it's a core part of how I work every single day.

2026 | Now

Building, Learning, Growing

Today I'm focused on frontend development, AI-assisted workflows, and building real projects that solve real problems. I'm not where I want to be yet — but I'm moving forward every single day, and that's enough.
